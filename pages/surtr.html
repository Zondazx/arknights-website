Surtr

"From now on I'll be working at Rhodes Island, hopefully you won't let me feel bored."

Whimsical, carefree, wandering by herself all day, no one knows what she's looking for. All they know is that she has a record book, filled with nonsensical location names. When she arrives at a place, she'd speak to herself, and cross out one of the names. She wouldn't belittle someone without reason, but her attitude during conversations is not friendly.

She's the best boss-killer in the game, due to her sheer magical DPS and her talents.
